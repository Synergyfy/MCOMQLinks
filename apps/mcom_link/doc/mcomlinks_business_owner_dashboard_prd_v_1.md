# PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS DOCUMENT (PRD)
## MCOMLINKS – Business Owner Dashboard

---

# 1. Purpose of the Business Owner Dashboard

The Business Owner Dashboard is where each business can:

- View their live offers
- See performance results
- Submit new offers
- Edit draft offers
- Track scans and claims
- Communicate with their assigned Agent

Important Principle:
Business Owners can manage their content.
They cannot control the rotator system.
They cannot change placement logic.

The dashboard must feel simple, clear, and empowering.

---

# 2. Login & Access

Each Business Owner must have:

- Secure login (email + password)
- Access only to their own business account
- If multiple branches, access only to their assigned locations

They must NOT see:
- Other businesses
- System-level controls
- Rotator settings

---

# 3. Dashboard Home Overview

When a Business Owner logs in, they must see:

Main Metrics (clearly displayed):

- Total Scans (current month)
- Total Claims (current month)
- Conversion Rate
- Active Offers Count
- Days remaining on current offer

Quick Action Buttons:

- Create New Offer
- View Offer Performance
- Contact Account Manager

This page must be simple and easy to understand.

---

# 4. Offer Management Section

Business Owners must be able to:

1. Create Offer Draft
2. Edit Draft Offer
3. Submit Offer for Approval
4. View Offer Status

Offer Creation Fields:

- Offer Title
- Short Description
- Offer Image Upload
- Call-To-Action Type (Visit Store / Show to Staff / Redeem Online)
- Start Date
- End Date

Offer Status Types:

- Draft
- Submitted
- Approved (Live)
- Rejected (with reason shown clearly)
- Expired

Important Rule:
Offer does NOT go live immediately.
It must be reviewed and approved by Admin.

If rejected, the reason must be visible with a clear message.

---

# 5. Live Offer View

For approved offers, Business Owner must see:

- Offer preview (how customers see it)
- Start and end date
- Performance metrics
- Option to request early termination (subject to Admin approval)

They must NOT be able to:
- Change offer once live (unless editing is allowed before review)
- Change rotator position

---

# 6. Performance & Analytics

Each Business Owner must have a performance page.

Performance per Offer:

- Total Impressions (if tracked)
- Total Scans
- Total Claims
- Conversion Rate

Performance over Time:

- Monthly scans chart
- Monthly claims chart

Data must be simple.
No complex technical terms.

The goal is to help them understand:
- Is my offer working?
- Are people engaging?

---

# 7. Notifications & Alerts

Business Owners must receive alerts for:

- Offer approved
- Offer rejected
- Offer expiring in 7 days
- Offer expired

Notifications should appear:

- Inside dashboard
- Optional email notification

---

# 8. Expiry & Renewal Flow

When an offer is about to expire:

System must:
- Show countdown (days remaining)
- Display reminder banner

After expiry:
- Offer automatically moves to "Expired"
- Business must create new offer to remain active in rotator

This ensures storefront stays fresh.

---

# 9. Account Information Section

Business Owners must be able to view (not fully edit unless allowed):

- Business Name
- Contact Person
- Email
- Phone
- Store Location
- Plan Type

Editable fields may include:
- Contact details

Major changes must require Agent or Admin approval.

---

# 10. Communication with Agent

Business Owners must have a simple messaging or request feature.

They should be able to:

- Send message to assigned Agent
- Request help
- Request performance advice

Conversation history must be visible.

---

# 11. Plan & Upgrade View (If Applicable)

If plan tiers exist (Basic / Premium), Business Owner should see:

- Current Plan
- Benefits of plan
- Option to request upgrade

Upgrade requests go to Agent/Admin for approval.

---

# 12. Restrictions & Controls

Business Owners must NOT be able to:

- Access Admin panel
- Access other businesses
- Change storefront layout
- Change rotator logic
- Directly publish offers without approval

All actions must be logged in system.

---

# 13. User Experience Principles

The Business Owner Dashboard must feel:

- Simple
- Clean
- Easy to understand
- Non-technical

They should feel:

- In control of their offers
- Informed about performance
- Supported by their Agent

It must not feel complicated.

---

# FINAL SUMMARY

The MCOMLINKS Business Owner Dashboard must:

1. Allow offer creation and submission
2. Show clear offer status
3. Display simple performance metrics
4. Provide expiry reminders
5. Allow communication with Agent
6. Restrict access to system controls
7. Encourage continuous offer renewal

Business Owners manage their promotions.
Agents manage the relationship.
Admin controls the system engine.

This dashboard gives businesses visibility and clarity without giving them control over the rotator logic.
