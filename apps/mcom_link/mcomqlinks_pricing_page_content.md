# MCOMQLinks Pricing Page

---

## 🟢 BASIC — “90-Day Access” (MANDATORY)

**Price:** £90 / 90 days  
**Tagline:** Start showing your business on MCOMQLinks

### What’s Included
- Claim and activate your Storefront listing
- Get your QR Brand ID
- Run MCOMQLinks campaigns
- Appear on external storefront campaigns (shop windows, posters)
- Appear on internal campaigns (in-store QR/NFC)
- Participate in National campaigns (managed by 247 GBS)
- Participate in Hyper local campaigns
- Use QR on VCards, posters, and campaign materials

### Limitations
- No promotion of third-party products/services
- No automatic renewal (expires after 90 days)
- No Expo access
- Standard visibility only

### Best For
- Businesses just getting started
- Testing the platform
- Local storefront presence

---

## 🔵 PRO — “Growth Mode”

**Price:** £450 / 90 days  
**Tagline:** Grow beyond your storefront and scale your campaigns

### What’s Included
- Everything in BASIC
- Promote third-party products & services
- Run Nearby campaigns (expand beyond your street)
- Auto 90-day rollover into next season
- Access to future seasonal campaigns
- Access to evergreen campaign cycles
- Greater campaign flexibility
- Increased exposure across network

### Key Advantage
Your campaigns continue automatically beyond 90 days

### Best For
- Businesses ready to scale
- Multi-product/service sellers
- Partner/collaboration businesses

---

## 🟣 PRO+ — “Full Visibility & Expo Access”

**Price:** £1100 / 90 days  
**Tagline:** Maximum exposure, priority access, and event promotion

### What’s Included
- Everything in PRO
- Full access to End-of-Season Marketing Expo
- Participate as seller or promoter
- Priority visibility in campaigns
- Higher placement in National campaigns
- Higher placement in Hyper local campaigns
- Premium positioning across MCOMQLinks
- Stronger brand exposure

### Key Advantage
You are actively promoted and highlighted across the network

### Best For
- Serious businesses
- Brands launching products/services
- Businesses that want maximum visibility

## 🔁 90-Day Seasonal Campaign System

- All plans run on a 90-day cycle
- Your business stays active only during this period
- After 90 days:
  - BASIC expires
  - PRO continues automatically
  - PRO+ continues automatically with priority benefits
